FAQ

Common Questions

How long does onboarding take?

Most hotels are fully live within 30 minutes. Our onboarding team guides you through every step, from room setup to billing configuration.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every hotel gets a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

Do you support multiple properties?

Absolutely. TRACKINN supports hotel chains and groups under a single account with a centralized admin dashboard. Contact our sales team for group pricing.

What payment methods do guests use?

We support all major UPI apps (PhonePe, GPay, BHIM), credit/debit cards via Razorpay, net banking, and cash payments. All transactions are GST-compliant.
