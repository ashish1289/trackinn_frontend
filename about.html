Our Story

We're Building the Future of Hospitality

TRACKINN was born out of frustration with clunky, expensive, outdated hotel software. We set out to build something that hotel owners in India actually love using.

Our Mission

Empowering Every Indian Hotelier

India has over 5 million hotel rooms spread across thousands of properties — from luxury 5-stars to small budget guesthouses in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities. Most of these properties still use pen-and-paper registers, disconnected spreadsheets, or expensive legacy software that wasn't built for Indian operations.

We built TRACKINN to change that. Our platform is designed ground-up for Indian hotel operators — with GST invoicing, Aadhaar KYC capture, Sarai API compliance, and UPI payment integration baked into every feature.

Our mission is simple: make world-class hotel management accessible to every Indian hotelier, regardless of size.
